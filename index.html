About Me

From Civil Engineer to Software Engineer

TL;DR: I'm Nick Orsi, a Software Engineer with civil engineering experience working on technically demanding, fast paced, dynamic projects looking to make an impact on the world at large.

Hi, my name is Nick Orsi! I'm a licensed Civil Engineer turned Software Engineer. For the past 8 years I worked for Kiewit, a nationally recognized private contractor building awesome construction projects. I managed critical operations that were technically demanding, fast paced, and susceptible to dynamic changes. Despite these challenges, my efforts consistently resulted in quality products completed safely, under budget, and on time. I am proud of the work I did for my employer, the clients, and for the communities the projects serve.

I have since decided to become a Software Engineer for several reasons. One is to pursue my passion for tech, which developed early in high school as I was exposed to science, math, and technology courses. I soon found myself learning intro to physics, chemistry, and even used 3D modeling software and 3D printers to build cool projects. Coupled with my interest in video games, I even explored going to college to become a video game developer, but decided to follow the footsteps of my Aunt and Grandfather to pursue a Civil Engineer degree.

The main reason I made this career change is to become an active contributor to an industry not bound to a particular work sector or geographical location. I see software engineering as an exciting chance to use my skillset once used to improve a particular region of the world to instead make a positive impact on the world at large. I plan to couple my previous experience working on technically demanding, fast paced, dynamic projects with my new skillset as a software engineer to continue to deliver quality products done safely, under budget, and on time.

I appreciate you visiting my site! While you’re here please take a look at my resume and some projects I’ve worked on!

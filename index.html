Construction Projects

GROSS RESERVOIR | Denver, CO | Project Info

The Gross Reservoir Expansion Project will raise the height of the existing dam by 131 feet to triple the reservoir's water storage capacity.

The project started in April of 2022 and is estimated to take 5 years to complete.

My primary role on this project was Lead Superintendent for the dam foundation excavation operation. This involved the rock excavation, rock slope anchor installation, and foundation preparation and cleaning along the entire new footprint of the dam.

SM 101 FREEWAY | Belmont, CA | Project Info

The San Mateo 101 Express Lanes Project added a new express lane in both the NB and SB directions of a 22 mile portion of the 101 freeway.

The project started in February of 2019 and was completed in March of 2023.

I held several roles on this project including Discipline Engineer and Roadway Excavation Superintendent. As the Discpline Engineer I was in charge of the internal documentation for all civil operations. As a Roadway Excavation Superintendent I managed crews executing the freeway widening work.

Oroville Spillway Repair | Oroville, CA | Project Info

The Oroville Emergency Spillway Repair Project completely rebuilt the main and emergency spillways as well as the recreational facilities.

The spillway repairs started in May of 2017 and was completed in November of 2018. The reconstruction of the recreational facilities continued into 2020.

I held numerous roles on this project starting as a Field Engineer and ending as a Superintendent. The operations I managed included blasting, spillway anchor installation, utility installation, and asphalt grinding/paving.
